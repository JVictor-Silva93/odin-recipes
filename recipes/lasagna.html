French Onion Mac and Cheese

A delicious Mac and Cheese dish with a taste of French onions!

An upscale mac and cheese! Don't skimp on the cheese! It makes the mac and cheese taste amazing!

Ingredients:

  • 4 Tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 Large Onions
  • Macaroni
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Bring water to a boiling point
  3. Put the ingredients in and cook
  4. Drain water and serve, wait untill cooled to eat.
