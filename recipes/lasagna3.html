lasagna

This is the world's best lasagna!

It takes a little work, but it is worth it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of sweet Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup of minced onions
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • Lasagna noodles

Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a boiling point
  2. Put the ingredients in and cook
  3. Drain water and serve, wait untill cooled to eat
