Pesto Pasta

A delicious pasta dish made with pesto sauce!

Easy to make, but full of flavor! Good hot or cold.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of pesto
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onions
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 package of pasta

Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a boiling point
  2. Put the ingredients in and cook
  3. Drain water and serve, wait untill cooled to eat
